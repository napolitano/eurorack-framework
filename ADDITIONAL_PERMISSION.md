## Permission

A natural person may manufacture, assemble, and distribute no more than five physical hardware units in total per calendar year that contain, execute, or were produced using this software, provided that every condition in this document is satisfied.

The five-unit limit applies collectively to the individual and to any company, organization, group, marketplace account, collaborator, or other party acting for, controlled by, or coordinated with that individual. The limit may not be multiplied through additional accounts, entities, collaborators, product names, repositories, marketplaces, or sales channels.

## Cost recovery only

Recipients may be charged no more than the documented direct costs actually attributable to the units distributed.

Permitted direct costs are limited to:

- printed circuit boards;
- electronic and mechanical components;
- third-party fabrication and assembly;
- front panels and enclosures;
- packaging and printed documentation;
- taxes and customs duties;
- payment-processing fees; and
- shipping costs actually paid for those units.

In addition to documented direct costs, an individual may charge a limited assembly allowance of no more than EUR 15 per completed physical unit, or the equivalent amount in another currency at the exchange rate applicable on the date of payment.

The assembly allowance may compensate only for:

- manual assembly;
- soldering;
- cleaning;
- visual inspection;
- firmware installation; and
- basic functional testing of the specific unit.

The assembly allowance may not be increased based on material cost, product value, market price, time spent, professional qualifications, local commercial rates, or design complexity.

The following may not be charged or recovered under this permission:

- development or design work;
- sourcing or procurement services;
- tools, equipment, depreciation, rent, utilities, administration, or other overhead;
- calibration services beyond basic functional testing;
- support, warranty, or after-sales service;
- risk premiums, contingency amounts, reserves, markups, royalties, commissions, or profit;
- bundled paid services, mandatory donations, subscriptions, sponsorships, or other direct or indirect benefits of monetary value.

The total assembly allowance charged under this permission may not exceed EUR 75 per natural person in any calendar year.

The activity must not be undertaken with an explicit or implicit intention to make a direct or indirect profit.

## Intended example - JLCPCB minimum order

A hobbyist who orders the usual minimum quantity of five PCBs from JLCPCB, assembles up to those five units, and passes them on at documented direct material, fabrication, tax, payment-processing, packaging, and shipping cost may additionally charge an assembly allowance of up to EUR 15 per completed unit.

Producing or distributing a sixth unit in the same calendar year, exceeding the annual assembly-allowance total of EUR 75, adding any further markup, repeatedly producing batches, or using the activity to promote, support, or operate a business is not covered by this permission and requires prior written authorization.
